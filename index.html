While we started out as being the nations Inflatable Experts, our rigorous training and careful choice of product lines has put us in the forefront of the boat sales, service, and fun industry in the Northeast. Explore our website to learn more about our great boat lines, impeccable service from highly trained professionals, and how to get involved in boating through our boat club

We sell and service the world's most reliable and efficient engine lines: Honda, Yamaha, and Tohatsu. Then, our factory trained technicians keep them running in tip-top shape for your worry-free boating.

Our technicians are factory trained and are regularly brought up to speed on the latest and the greatest tools an techniques to keep you on the water instead of in the shop ( or worse, parked in the backyard). Whether you need a tune up or an overhaul, Maritime Solutions is the reliable choice.
We also provide launch, haul and storage services.
